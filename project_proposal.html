Hyravel

Vehicle Rental Management System

A Little Description About Project

This project implements Hyravel, an automobile renting web that provides a platform for people to rent two-wheel as well as four-wheel rides on rent. Hyravel possesses a couple of distinctive features that makes it unique in the market of vehicle renting services. One of these features is that with renting a car for themselves. Like Uber and other riding sharing services, Hyravel gets together its users for their personal vehicle. With the feature of renting the automobile for customized city tours. Hyravel makes itself a unique introduction in the ride-sharing as well as the vehicle renting industry. Similar to traditional Rent-A-Car service, a user can rent the automobile and drive itself to any destination at any time and day. Users can also rent the automobiles for special events like parties, weddings, solo and group rides, etc.
